In 2021, after getting covid, I ended up dealing with lingering symptoms that pushed me into reading PubMed for the first time.I was trying to understand what was happening, but I found that scientific papers came with jargon and the dense PubMed UI.I'd use ChatGPT to simplify studies, and eventually built OpenScience.ink as a more structured version of this.At the moment, it scans PubMed every morning for new papers across 8 topics: • Long Covid • Circadian Biology • Psychedelic Science • CRISPR • GLP-1s • Gut-Brain Axis • Longevity & Aging • mRNA TechnologyAnd every week (on Mondays), it sends a newsletter in each topic with the most relevant studies from the past week.The idea is to make it easier to keep up with new research in plain English.Open to any thoughts or ideas!
