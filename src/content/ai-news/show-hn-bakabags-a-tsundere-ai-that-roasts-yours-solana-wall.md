BakaBags is a small experiment I built over the last week.You paste a public Solana wallet address, the app reads the wallet’s holdings and current market context, and an AI character reacts to the portfolio in a sharp, screenshotable way.Important: it only reads public wallet data. There is no wallet connect, no signing, and no private key access.The idea was to combine onchain data with personality instead of building yet another sterile dashboard. So it sits somewhere between wallet analysis, entertainment, and a shareable crypto-native toy.I’m mainly curious about two things:- whether the “personality + live wallet data” concept feels interesting at all - whether this reads as a gimmick, or as a product direction with real potentialWould love blunt feedback.
