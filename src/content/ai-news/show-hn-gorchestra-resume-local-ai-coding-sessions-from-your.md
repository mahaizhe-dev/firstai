i've been dogfooding some version of this idea for a few weeks now and i'd thought i'd share my re-implementation from this weekend. basically it's a webserver that controls and unlimited amount of codex (preferred) or claude agents. it allows me to context switch more easily, as well as kick off tasks and instruct agents while i'm away from my desk. the storage is sqlite and persistent, regardless of agent status. for codex you can upload images and queue messages just like the CLI. i'm on mac so i've streamlined installation via brew, but open an issue if you have problems on another platform. hope someone finds it useful!
