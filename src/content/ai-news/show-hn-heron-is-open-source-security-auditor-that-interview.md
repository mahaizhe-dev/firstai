Last week security guy in our company asked me to write doc with information about how they work, which data and systems it touches for all my AI agents. "It's too much work" i thought. So i built Heron. It interviews your agent with 10 main questions and smart follow-ups if feel uncertainty. It also has regulatory flags (SOC2, GDPR, EU AI Act etc). As a result you get a full report with all needed information.Feel free to try. No SDK, no code changes, MIT licensed. Have 4 modes (server, override, scan, skill). Would love feedback and PRs.
