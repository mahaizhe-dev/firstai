Every AI is trained on 1-on-1 chats. But in a 1-on-1 there's no reason to disagree. AI just mirrors you back. That's not intelligence, that's flattery with extra steps. Put AI in a room with 30 people who disagree. Now agreeing with one means dismissing another. Suddenly it has to actually think. Nobody seems to be doing this. Why?
