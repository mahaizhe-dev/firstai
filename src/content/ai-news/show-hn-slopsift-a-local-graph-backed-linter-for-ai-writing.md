SlopSift uses NLP-backed lint rules. It goes beyond word lists and part-of-speech tags by matching relationships in a dependency graph. Though it still includes a few word-based rules for tells like “delve” and, most recently, “load-bearing.”It’s deterministic and runs locally, in your browser or from the CLI.
