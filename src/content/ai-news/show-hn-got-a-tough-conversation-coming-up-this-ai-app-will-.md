An interactive tool designed for self-discovery around managing difficult conversations. You enter a topic, choose a part of the conversation to explore (preparation, emotional handling, and closure) and the app proposes a method from a bank of 15 and applies it for you. You can then continue the journey until you feel ready. The app saves your examples so you end up with a personalized “conversation guidebook.”Its a new kind of lightweight learning app, built with Claude in an afternoon, based on some research I did for a workshop.
