We’ve all seen the explosion of AI tools lately. Every day there are dozens of new launches claiming to be the next revolution.After a while it becomes hard to tell what is actually useful and what is just another vibecoded landing page.So we built Glad-AI-Tor.It’s a simple arena where AI tools are submitted and exposed to the crowd. People can roast them, judge them, and decide if they deserve attention.No paid placement. No marketing tricks. Just builders putting their tools in front of the internet and seeing what survives.Some tools get destroyed. Some unexpectedly rise.Curious to see what happens when the crowd decides.
