Cronbox is a cron, where an AI agent wakes up and works on a schedule. We believe a lot of repetitive tasks can benefit a lot from AI agents. And scheduling them in the cloud keeps you hands-free.You can do things like, taking screenshot of a site, resize it to size 1000 x 1000, and look for price changes on the page and get notified via email.Or ask it to draw svg of a 'pelican riding a bicyle' whenever a new model is released on openai and mail it to you.Every job runs in its own linux sandbox, so a lot more things are possible.While Claude's cloud scheduling is limited by a restrictive sandbox and no screenshot or SVG support, cronbox.sh provides a full power environment with unrestricted libraries, curl, and native web capturing.
