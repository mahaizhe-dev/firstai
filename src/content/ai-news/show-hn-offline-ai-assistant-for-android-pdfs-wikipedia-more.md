Hello,This an app for offgriders to use in situations without internet. Permits to download wikipedia and to make questions over the folders with books that you have on the storage of the phone.Other features are the offline maps, where it permits to ask the bot about points of interest in a specific region and these maps stay in the storage to be used later when needed.In addition contains other actions such as music player, akin to alexa sans internet so you can play musics using your voice (assistant mode) or just write about the mood of music you want to play.Feedback is welcome.
