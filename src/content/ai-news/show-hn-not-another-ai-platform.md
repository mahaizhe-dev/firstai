I'm 15 and this is my AI workspace platform that I have building as a side-project. I really wanted an AI platform that had all the latest features like tool-calling, web search, advanced reasoning etc. along with really nice ui/ux - like custom elements for email drafts, flashcards, instructions and checklists that not only stand out from all the paragraphs of text we are familiar with AI but these "cards" or "widgets" are also interactive to the user.Instead of waiting for something like that to exist, I started building it myself.That became HelloAI.It supports the latest AI models, but really what stands out is the richer output formats designed to make AI outputs more usable instead of just long paragraphs.It's still independent and something I actively use and keep improving based on real usage and feedback.I'd appreciate any feedback on the idea, UX, or anything you think could be improved.
