Due to the recent conflict in the Middle East, my boss half-laid me off (told me not come to work but didn't officially fire me). I had free time to spend or waste. Spent about 4 weeks to build this open-source tool which holds the flag for the agent as a service move. I wanted it to be simple and easy that small businesses can build an AI agent for their service in natural language using the domain knowledge and data they have. It can be used between a deploying a booking appointment agent on Whatsapp to actually selling a real stuff; as the tool allows agents to take Stripe payment. WhatsApp and Telegram are best spots to deploy. Token consumption is fair compared to OpenClaw, I recommend using DeepSeek as the provider. Use Claude if you are selling something expensive!
